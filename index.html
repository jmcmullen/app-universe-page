Safe for students

App Universe comes free on the latest Android build, or you can download it using the link above. Open it up and take a look around! You'll find all kinds of different apps to help augment your student's classroom experience.

Feel free to install it on student machines, we curate every app to make sure they're safe for kids.

Navigate with ease

From the front page, you can see apps that are available for download, apps that you've installed already, and apps that are waiting to update. Simply press the tabs, or swipe left and right on the main screen to move between sections.

Simple installs

Once you've found an app you'd like to try it - simply click the install button in the top right! App Universe will download it straight away and begin the app installation process.

Once it's done, you'll be able to find the app in your app drawer under the XO icon on the home screen.

You can also update, uninstall and run apps from the app page once it's installed.

XO-id gives you more

Sign-in with XO-id in order to get access to even more apps from our partners!

As a teacher, signing in with XO-id also gives you access to the Google Play store - which means you can try out any app that Android has to offer.
